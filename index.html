\title{Research Statement}
\author{Shuai Wei}
\address{Clemson University}
\date{Version: Aug 08, 2021}
\maketitle

I use wimplicial complex to study non-square free monomial ideals, for example, ideals of the form $(X_i^aX_j^a,X_k^bX_\ell^b,\dots)$ with $a,b,\dots \in \mathbb N$. These ideals can be constructed from for example a weighted finite simple graph.
