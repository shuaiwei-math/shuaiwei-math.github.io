Number theory.
    Let 
    \begin{equation}
        \pi_i(x) = \# \{p = x \mid p \text{ is prime}\}. 
    \end{equation}
    Then
    \begin{equation}
        \pi(x) \sim \int_2^{x} \frac{1}{ln t}dt.
    \end{equation}
